Hello you!
You are probably wondering what this is and how can this extra gift be even better than this amazing book. Well, I can’t guarantee that this website is better, in fact the book took waaaaaay longer to write than the code for this website. This is a different type of gift, I wanted to showcase the skills I have learned on this journey so far and I can't think of a better way to do so than honouring you in the process.
A tip from me, I advise starting with the video below. Yes, I see there is this big button saying 'My Message to You' above the video, I know it's there and you probably want to push it but just wait a tiny bit. Watch the video first then you can come back to the message after.
Will you do this for me? Promise? Okay good.
The Video
You made it! If you have listened to my advise, which I hope you have seeing as I don't think I have ever given a bad piece of advice. Then you have found yourself at the video.
This video will explain the entire website and navigating it in the correct order (remember that was a tip above but its more of a demand). What you will watch now is completely recorded and edited by myself, I think I did this to force myself to get better in front of a camera.
You can let me know how I did.
By the way, I am not trying to make it as an actor but constructive criticism is accepted. Unless its horrible, then keep it to yourself.
